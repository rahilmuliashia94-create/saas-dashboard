Our Story

A Company Built on Trust

Everest Security Pvt. Ltd. was founded in 2003 with a vision to bring world-class security solutions to India. Starting from a small office in Rajkot, Gujarat, we have grown to become one of the most trusted names in the security industry.

Our partnership with leading Israeli security technology companies has allowed us to offer cutting-edge solutions that meet international standards. Today, we serve over 5000 clients across multiple sectors including banking, logistics, industrial, and residential.
